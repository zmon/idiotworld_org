Having said that, you won’t find much to get stuck on here at idiotworld.org. I prefer to move as quickly as possible past the whining, boss-bating, -blaming, and -bashing to an exciting new development in collaborative leadership. The number one reason people act like idiots in the workplace is fear. The most effective way to idiot-proof your organization is to align each team member’s dominant Leadership Style and Motivation to his or her assigned responsibilities. That way, everybody acts like a leader every day.

The old school model called for the anointing of high-performing, seniority-soured, or brown-nosed individuals to be sent through expensive leadership training and return as leaders. More improbable was the old school expectation that the rest of us would, by osmosis, become loyal and dedicated followers. Old school leadership = us vs. them.

The new school leadership model cultivates a culture in which everybody acts like a leader all the time. The leadership training is quick and easy: Lead the way you like to be led. Any questions? Fear produces defensive and compensatory behavior. Resonance between Leadership Style and responsibilities produces confident and productive performance.
